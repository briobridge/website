Our Story

From Vision to a Global Services Company

Brio Bridge was founded by Hassan Ali Qureshi with a clear mission: to make world-class business services accessible to companies of every size, across every industry, anywhere in the world.

Starting from Lahore, Pakistan, we've grown into a multi-vertical operation spanning technology, engineering, logistics, BPO, finance, and HR — with clients across North America, the Middle East, Europe, and beyond.

We believe that geography should never be a barrier to quality. Our model combines the cost advantage of offshore talent with the discipline, communication, and delivery standards of the world's best service firms.

All sales and service fees paid to Brio Bridge are final and non-refundable. Due to the nature of professional services — which involve immediate allocation of personnel, resources, infrastructure, and expertise upon engagement — Brio Bridge does not offer refunds under ordinary circumstances. This applies to all divisions: Technology, Engineering, Logistics, BPO, Finance, and HR. By initiating a payment, you acknowledge that services represent a binding commitment of Brio Bridge resources and that compensation is earned upon engagement commencement, not upon project completion.

2. Payment Terms

(a) Project-based engagements require a non-refundable deposit of 50–100% prior to commencement; (b) Monthly retainers are billed in advance and non-refundable once the billing period begins; (c) Invoices are due upon receipt; (d) Late payments incur a finance charge of 1.5% per month (18% per annum); (e) Brio Bridge may suspend services immediately upon non-payment without liability.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Absolutely non-refundable under all circumstances: (a) Onboarding, setup, and consultation fees; (b) Any work commenced, including drafts, prototypes, or research; (c) Retainer fees for any commenced billing period; (d) Third-party costs, licenses, software, or ad spend incurred on your behalf; (e) Rush fees; (f) Any deliverable delivered, reviewed, or accessed by the Client; (g) Services paused due to Client failure to provide materials, access, or approvals.

4. Limited Exception — Non-Delivery

A service credit (at Brio Bridge's sole discretion) may be considered only if: Brio Bridge has completely failed to commence any work within 30 calendar days of the agreed start date through no fault of the Client, and written notice is provided within 5 business days thereafter. Any credit shall be limited to the uncommitted portion of fees paid and issued as a service credit — not a cash refund. This exception does not apply where delays result from Client failure to provide information or approvals, force majeure events, or scope changes.

5. Cancellation Policy

(a) Before work commences: deposit is forfeited; no liability for remaining fees. (b) After commencement: Client is liable for all work to date at standard rates plus third-party costs incurred. (c) Monthly retainers require 30 days written notice; fees during notice period are non-refundable. (d) Brio Bridge may terminate any engagement for cause — including non-payment, breach of contract, or misrepresentation — without refund and without liability.

6. Dispute Resolution
