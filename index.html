Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025 · Effective: January 1, 2025

BrioBridge is committed to delivering high-quality services and ensuring client satisfaction. This policy outlines the terms under which refunds or cancellations may be requested.

1. Project-Based Services

  • Before work begins: Full refund of any deposit if cancelled in writing within 48 hours of payment.
  • After work has commenced: Refunds prorated based on percentage of work completed at time of cancellation.
  • After full delivery: No refunds once a project has been fully delivered and accepted. If deliverables do not meet the agreed scope, BrioBridge will provide revisions at no additional cost.

2. Monthly Retainer & Ongoing Services

  • A minimum of 30 days written notice is required to cancel any monthly retainer service.
  • Fees paid for the current billing period are non-refundable.
  • No further charges will be made after the cancellation notice period is fulfilled.

3. Service Quality Disputes

If unsatisfied with the quality of work, contact us within 7 business days of receiving the deliverable with specific feedback. BrioBridge will make every reasonable effort to resolve concerns through revisions before a refund is considered.

4. Non-Refundable Items

  • Consultation fees and discovery call preparation costs
  • Work that has been delivered, accepted, and deployed or used by the client
  • Third-party costs incurred on behalf of the client
  • Rush or expedited delivery fees

5. Refund Process

To request a refund, email hello@briobridge.com with your name, invoice number, date of payment, and reason. Approved refunds are processed within 10 business days via the original payment method.
